Tribute to Sachin Tendulkar

Master Blaster of Cricket

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. Hailed as the world's most prolific batsman of all time, he is the all-time highest run-scorer in both ODI and Test cricket with more than 18,000 runs and 15,000 runs, respectively. He also holds the record for receiving the most player of the match awards in international cricket. Tendulkar was a Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha by nomination from 2012 to 2018.
This tribute page celebrates his remarkable career and contributions to the sport.

Cricket Career

Sachin Tendulkar made his international debut for India at the age of 16 and went on to achieve numerous records throughout his illustrious career. Sachin Tendulkar, hailed as the "Master Blaster," began his cricket career at the age of 16, making his international debut for India in 1989. Over a span of 24 years, he amassed the highest run total in international cricket, setting records with 100 international centuries and being the first player to reach 10,000 runs in ODIs. His exceptional skill, technique, and unwavering passion for the game earned him widespread acclaim, making Sachin an iconic figure in cricket history.

Achievements

Sachin Tendulkar, often referred to as the "God of Cricket," boasts a stellar array of achievements. Notably, he is the first player to score 100 international centuries, reaching the pinnacle of cricket with an extraordinary career that includes being the leading run-scorer in both Test and ODI formats. His outstanding contributions led to the conferral of the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, recognizing his unparalleled impact on the sport.



The most popular quote of him is:
"I have never tried to compare myself to anyone else."

Social Work

Sachin Tendulkar is actively engaged in philanthropic efforts, particularly in the domains of education, healthcare, and child welfare. His social work includes supporting initiatives that aim to make a positive impact on the lives of people, reflecting his commitment to contributing beyond the boundaries of the cricket field.

Personal Life

Sachin Tendulkar, born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India, is married to Anjali Tendulkar. The couple has two children, Sara and Arjun, and Sachin's personal life reflects a balance between his iconic cricket career and his roles as a husband and father. Sachin Tendulkar's parents are Ramesh Tendulkar and Rajni Tendulkar. They played a crucial role in supporting Sachin's early cricketing journey, and their encouragement and guidance contributed to the development of one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.
